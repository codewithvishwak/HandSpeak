HandSpeak is an innovative web application designed to bridge the communication gap between the deaf, hard of hearing, and those unfamiliar with sign language. Using advanced gesture recognition technology, the app translates sign language into text and converts it into natural speech in real time.

Our goal is to make conversations smoother, more inclusive, and accessible for everyone. Whether it’s daily interactions, education, or professional communication, HandSpeak empowers users to express themselves freely and be understood instantly.

With a simple interface and powerful technology, HandSpeak transforms the way we connect—breaking barriers and giving voice to every hand movement.
